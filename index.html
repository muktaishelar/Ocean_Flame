About Us

Ocean Flame combines the best vegetarian, non-vegetarian and seafood dishes from around the world.
Ocean Flame brings together the finest vegetarian, non-vegetarian, and seafood delicacies from around the world, crafted with passion and served with excellence.
At Ocean Flame, every dish is prepared using fresh, high-quality ingredients, authentic recipes, and carefully selected spices to deliver rich flavors and memorable dining experiences. From sizzling seafood platters and premium meat specialties to delicious vegetarian creations, our diverse menu is designed to satisfy every palate.
We are committed to exceptional hospitality, elegant ambiance, and outstanding service, ensuring that every visit feels special. Whether you're enjoying a family dinner, celebrating a special occasion, hosting a business gathering, or simply exploring new flavors, Ocean Flame offers the perfect setting for unforgettable moments.
